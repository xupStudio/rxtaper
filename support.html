Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by your device's app store. We cannot cancel them for you.

iOS (iPhone):
  • Go to Settings > Tap your name > Subscriptions > RxTaper > Cancel Subscription.

Android:
  • Open Google Play Store > Tap Profile Icon (top right) > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > RxTaper > Cancel subscription.
Is my voice data saved?
No. Your voice is sent securely to our AI for transcription and summarization, but the raw audio file is not permanently stored on our servers after processing.
Can I export my data for my doctor?
Yes! Go to the "History" tab or the "Insights" page. You can view your stability trends and summaries which are designed to be shared with healthcare providers.
Is this app a replacement for a doctor?
Absolutely not. RxTaper is a tracking and journaling tool. Always consult your doctor before making any changes to your medication dosage.

常見問題 (FAQ)

我該如何取消訂閱？

訂閱服務由 Apple 或 Google 統一管理，我們無法直接為您取消。

iOS (iPhone) 用戶：
  • 前往 iPhone 的「設定」> 點擊您的姓名 >「訂閱項目」> 選擇 RxTaper > 取消訂閱。

Android 用戶：
  • 開啟 Google Play 商店 > 點擊右上角個人頭像 > 付款與訂閱 > 訂閱內容 > 選擇 RxTaper > 取消訂閱。
我的語音檔案會被儲存嗎？
不會。您的錄音僅會加密傳送至 AI 進行轉錄與摘要，處理完成後，我們不會在伺服器上永久保存您的原始音訊檔案。
我可以匯出資料給醫生看嗎？
可以！請前往「歷史紀錄」或「洞察」頁面。我們的圖表與 AI 摘要專為醫病溝通設計，您可以直接出示手機畫面與醫師討論。
這個 App 可以取代醫生嗎？
絕對不行。RxTaper 僅作為紀錄與追蹤工具。在調整任何藥物劑量之前，請務必諮詢您的主治醫師。
